Our Services

Meca schools specialize fully or in part in educating students how to repair, build or maintain vehicles, including motorcycles, cars and trucks. While meca programs don't typically include liberal arts topics such as history and literature, classes in mathematics and writing may be required.


Each listed meca schools may offer different levels of professional education and they may also expect students to meet rigorous educational requirements in order to complete a program. Some schools also specialize in specific types of automotive programs such as:


  • Small engine or motorcycle repair
  • Diesel engines
  • Gasoline engines
  • Body work and collision repair
  • High-performance tuning

Some schools may only have regional campuses or they may have online offerings. Generally speaking, while online programs can provide the needed classroom work required to earn a degree or certificate of completion, schools are still likely to require students to complete hands-on training courses.
